About

I am a senior at the University of Wyoming persuing a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Visual Communication Design. I specialize in graphic design, including digital works on Procreate, Photoshop, and Illustator. My work tends to be family oriented as well as inspired by fictional films/stories.

Graphic Designer & Artist.

I specialize in digital art, though I also enjoy working with traditional mediums such as paint and charcoal. I increasingly turn to digital software to craft my illustrations, finding it a versatile tool for bringing my ideas to life.
